## The brief

The client wanted to move from a static showcase to an interactive
experience where every user could assemble their own "pack" and place
an order.

## What we did

- Refreshed brand identity with new guidelines
- Real-time product configurator
- Integration with the existing order system
- Bilingual IT/EN site driven by filesystem content

## Outcome

+38% conversion in the first three months, average ticket up 22%.
