## The project

Nexus Core is one of our internal products: a platform to orchestrate
workflows across distributed teams, designed to be quick to learn and
pleasant to use every day.

## What we did

- Product strategy and roadmap
- Complete design system (tokens, components, motion)
- Frontend implementation in Nuxt + Vue
- Filesystem-first backend on Nitro

## Outcome

A solid foundation to grow the product on, and a reusable design system
for the projects that came next.
