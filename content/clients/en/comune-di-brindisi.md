## The partner

The Comune di Brindisi is the institutional authority of the city of
Brindisi, capital of its province in the Puglia region of southern Italy.
With over 80,000 residents, it is one of the main centres of the lower
Adriatic coast, a historic port and a cultural and economic hub for
the surrounding territory.

## What we are doing together

We are partners on CityFix, our platform for taking care of the city.
Brindisi is the first municipality we are building and calibrating the
product with on a real case: reporting flows, operators, suppliers,
triage scenarios and priority handling.
